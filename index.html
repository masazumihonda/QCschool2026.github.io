New computational methods in quantum field theory 2026




26th(Mon) - 28th(Wed) January, 2026
RIKEN Wako Campus, Main Research Building 435-437








On this school

近年、量子コンピュータやその周辺の理論的・技術的な発展が著しく、素粒子・原子核分野においても「場の理論の新しい計算方法」として注目を集めています。

Main targets of this school are graduate students and postdocs. This school provides opportunities to discuss recent research trends and their applications through lectures by experts and presentations by participants.

Themes

  • Quantum computing
  • Quantum chaos
  • Quantum error correction
  • Quantum many body system
  • Numerical simulation of quantum field theory
  • Holography and Quantum infomation

Lecturers

  • Junichi Haruna (Osaka University) "Introduction to Quantum Error Correction (tentative)"
  • Yoshimasa Hidaka (Yukawa Institute for Theoretical Physics/RIKEN iTHEMS) “Introduction to Hamiltonian Lattice Gauge Theory (tentative)”
  • Tokiro Numasawa (Tokyo University) "Open Majorana system (tentative)"
